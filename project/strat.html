Strategy

Trading

Trading is an essential tool for success is CS:GO. Trading is the idea of pushing together with teammates so that if an enemy kills one, other teammates can kill that enemy.

Terrorist

Rushing

Choose a site and run towards it as a team at full speed with your knife out, attempting to push the site before defenders have a chance to prepare.

Playing for Picks

The team spreads out to strategic entry/exit points across the map to wait for a Counter Terrorist push, playing to take out the pushers and take the site from where they exited.

Counter Terrorist

Falling Back

After spotting enemies rushing you should fall back immediately after intial contact and give your teammates the information so they can back you up.

Pushing

If the location of the enemy team is unknown you can push in order to gain information. If the location of the enemy team is known, pushing can be a good way to get a good flanking position.
